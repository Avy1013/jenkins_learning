Learn Jenkins - Your Path to Automation!

What is Jenkins?

Jenkins is a free and open-source automation server. It helps you automate the software development lifecycle (SDLC) tasks, including building, testing, and deploying your applications.

Learn More

Why Learn Jenkins?

Learning Jenkins offers several benefits:

  • Increased efficiency and productivity
  • Improved software quality and consistency
  • Reduced manual effort and human error

Getting Started with Jenkins

Learning Jenkins is easier than you think! Here's a roadmap:

  1. Install Jenkins on your system.
  2. Explore the Jenkins user interface.
  3. Create your first simple pipeline.
  4. Explore plugins to extend Jenkins functionalities.
